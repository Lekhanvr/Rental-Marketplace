How CampusShare works

1

List or discover items

Lenders create listings with price, deposit, and availability. Borrowers search and request items.

2

Approve & pay deposit

Lenders approve requests. Borrowers confirm with a security deposit and pick up on campus.

3

Rate & resolve issues

After return, both sides rate each other. Disputes can be raised through the resolution center.
